## Design Considerations

### Tamil Calendar Integration
- **Traditional Dating**: Use Tamil month names and traditional calendar system
- **Dual Display**: Show both Tamil and Gregorian dates
- **Lunar Phases**: Include moon phase indicators for relevant festivals
- **Regional Variations**: Account for different regional celebration dates

### Cultural Authenticity
- **Proper Terminology**: Use authentic Tamil names for festivals
- **Religious Sensitivity**: Respectful presentation of sacred information
- **Traditional Colors**: Use colors associated with specific festivals
- **Inclusive Approach**: Cover festivals from various Tamil traditions

### Practical Usability
- **Offline Calendar**: Cache festival dates for offline access
- **Smart Notifications**: Contextual reminders based on location and preferences
- **Integration**: Link with device calendar apps
- **Accessibility**: Screen reader friendly festival descriptions
